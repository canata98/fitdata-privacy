📱 What is FitData?

FitData is your personal AI fitness coach for iOS featuring:

  • Workout Tracking: Track your training sessions in real-time
  • AI Workout Plan Creation: Personalized plans based on your goals
  • Intelligent Analysis: AI-powered performance evaluation and recommendations
  • Offline Usage: Full functionality without internet connection
  • iCloud Sync: Automatic synchronization between your devices

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I create a new workout plan?
A: Go to the "Workout Plans" section and tap the "+" symbol. You can either manually create a plan or ask the AI coach for help.
Q: How does the AI trainer work?
A: The AI trainer analyzes your workout data and provides personalized recommendations. Ask questions like "How was my training last week?" or "Create a 3-day plan for me".
Q: Are my data synchronized?
A: Yes, your data is automatically synchronized via iCloud and Firebase. This gives you access to your workouts on all your devices.
Q: Does the app work offline?
A: Absolutely! You can track workouts, use plans, and enter data even without an internet connection. Synchronization happens automatically once you're back online.
Q: How do I delete my account and all data?
A: Go to Settings → Account → "Delete Account". Alternatively, contact us via email for complete data deletion.
