1. Overview

FitData is an iOS app for personal fitness tracking. We take your data protection seriously and process your information exclusively to provide app functionality.

2. What Data Do We Collect?

  • Workout Data: Training sessions, exercises, weights, repetitions
  • Workout Plans: User-created or AI-generated workout plans
  • App Usage: Basic usage statistics to improve the app
  • AI Chat: Your messages with the AI trainer (only to improve responses)

3. How Do We Use Your Data?

  • Providing core functionality (workout tracking, plan creation)
  • AI-based training recommendations and chat responses
  • Synchronization between your devices via iCloud
  • Improving app performance and user experience

4. Data Storage

  • Local: Your data is primarily stored on your iPhone
  • iCloud: Automatic synchronization via your private iCloud account
  • Firebase: Secure cloud storage for backup and cross-device sync

5. Data Sharing

We do NOT share your personal data with third parties. Exceptions:

  • Technical service providers (Firebase/Google) only for app functionality
  • Legal obligations (only when legally required)

6. Your Rights

  • Delete Data: Via app settings or account deletion
  • Export Data: Workout data can be exported
  • Data Access: Contact us for data access requests

7. Data Security

We use modern encryption technologies and secure cloud infrastructures. Your data is protected by Apple's and Google's security standards.

8. Children Under 13

FitData is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.
