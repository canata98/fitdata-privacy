1. Overview

At FitData, we take your privacy seriously. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly, including:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, profile details
  • Fitness Data: Workouts, exercises, sets, weights, duration
  • Apple Health Integration: Data from Apple Health (with your permission)
  • AI Chat Conversations: Interactions and usage patterns
  • App Usage: Analytics and performance metrics
  • Device Information: For troubleshooting and optimization

3. AI Chat Data Processing

When using AI chat features:

  • Your workout data is processed to provide personalized guidance
  • Conversations are encrypted and securely stored
  • Data is processed by trusted AI providers under strict privacy agreements
  • You control which training plans and exercises are shared with the AI
  • Conversations are stored for service quality improvement and context
  • All AI processing complies with applicable privacy laws

4. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide and improve our fitness tracking services
  • Generate AI-powered personalized fitness recommendations
  • Seamlessly sync data across your devices
  • Analyze usage patterns to enhance user experience
  • Provide customer support and troubleshooting
  • Send important service updates and notifications
  • Ensure service security and prevent misuse

5. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not sell your personal data. We may share data under these limited circumstances:

  • With your explicit consent
  • With trusted service providers (AI processing, cloud storage) under strict privacy agreements
  • With Apple Health (if you grant permission)
  • For legal compliance or to protect our rights
  • In anonymized, aggregated form for research and analytics

6. Data Security and Retention

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • End-to-end encryption for sensitive data
  • Secure cloud storage with regular backups
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Restricted team access to personal data
  • Data retention policies to remove unnecessary information
  • Secure deletion upon account deletion

7. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access and download your data
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete your account and data
  • Opt out of analytics and crash reporting
  • Control AI chat data usage
  • Revoke Apple Health permissions
  • Receive data in a portable format

Contact us at fit.data1@gmail.com to exercise these rights.
