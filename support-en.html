FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the most common questions.

Does FitData work offline?
Yes — FitData is offline-first. You can log workouts, view your plans, and track sets without any internet connection. Data syncs automatically when you're back online. Only AI Chat requires a connection.
Does my data sync across devices?
Yes. Log in with the same account on any device and your workouts, plans, and progress sync automatically. Offline changes sync as soon as you're back online.
What's included in FitData Pro?
FitData Pro includes: AI Workout Coach with 80+ monthly conversations, unlimited workout plans, advanced performance analytics, full workout history, calendar view, and priority support.
How do I manage my subscription?
Manage your profile under Settings → Account. For Pro subscriptions, go to Settings → Subscription or use your device's App Store settings. You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel anytime through the App Store.
How does Apple Health integration work?
FitData integrates seamlessly with Apple Health to sync your workouts, close activity rings, and provide a complete fitness picture. Grant permissions under Settings → Permissions for full integration.
How does workout tracking work?
Start workouts from your plans or create custom sessions. Log sets with weight, reps, or duration in real time. Built-in rest timers keep you on track. All data is saved automatically.
How can I track my progress?
FitData offers detailed analytics: exercise progress charts, strength trends over time, workout consistency metrics, and personal record tracking. AI chat can analyze your progress and suggest improvements.
How is my health data protected?
We use industry-standard encryption, never share personal health data with third parties without your consent, and securely store AI conversations. Review our full Privacy Policy for details.
What is AI Chat and how does it work?
AI Chat is your personal fitness coach powered by advanced AI. It analyzes your workout data, creates personalized workout plans, and provides expert guidance. Pro users get 80–100 conversations per month, free users receive around 5.
Why are there AI Chat limits?
AI processing requires significant computing resources. Limits ensure sustainable service for all users — Pro users receive enough conversations for daily coaching and plan building.
Troubleshooting

Common fixes

Sync Issues

  • Check your internet connection
  • Make sure you're logged in with the same account
  • Log out and back into the app

AI Chat Issues

  • Internet connection required for AI features
  • Link relevant workout plans for better answers
  • Check your monthly conversation limit
