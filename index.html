FAQ

Common questions

Does FitData work offline?
Yes — FitData is offline-first. You can log workouts, view plans, and track sets without any internet connection. Data syncs automatically when you're back online. Only AI Chat requires a connection.
What's included in FitData Pro?
Pro unlocks: AI Personal Trainer with 80+ monthly conversations, unlimited training plans, advanced performance analytics, full workout history, calendar view, and priority support.
How does Apple Health integration work?
FitData reads and writes to Apple Health with your permission. Completed workouts automatically close your activity rings, and HealthKit data enriches your analytics inside FitData.
Is my AI chat data private?
Yes. AI conversations are encrypted and processed by trusted providers under strict privacy agreements. You control which plans the AI can see, and you can delete your history anytime.
Does FitData sync across devices?
Yes. Log in with the same account on any device and your workouts, plans, and progress sync automatically. Offline changes sync as soon as you're back online.
How do I cancel my subscription?
Manage or cancel your Pro subscription anytime through iOS Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions. No hoops to jump through.
