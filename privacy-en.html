2. General Information on Data Processing

We process personal data exclusively in accordance with the GDPR. Processing only takes place when there is a legal basis.

  • to perform a contract under Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR
  • based on your consent under Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR
  • based on legitimate interests under Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR

3. Categories of Data Processed

FitData processes only data required for app operation, sync, analysis, and stability.

  • Account data: Email address and display name. Purpose: user account and authentication. Legal basis: Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR.
  • Training and fitness data: Workout logs, exercises, repetitions, weights, and training plans. Purpose: app functionality. Legal basis: Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR.
  • Health data: Steps, heart rate, and calories. Purpose: analysis. Legal basis: Art. 9(2)(a) GDPR and Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR. Consent can be withdrawn at any time.
  • Location data: GPS data, distance, pace, and duration. Purpose: run and activity tracking. Legal basis: Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR.
  • AI chat data: Messages sent to the AI. Purpose: response generation. Legal basis: Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR.
  • Device data: Device, operating system, and app version. Purpose: stability, diagnostics, and error analysis. Legal basis: Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR.

4. Purposes of Processing

We use personal data to operate and improve FitData.

  • providing app functionality
  • syncing your data across devices
  • improving and stabilizing the app
  • error analysis and technical diagnostics
  • AI support and response generation

No advertising. No data sales.

5. Third-Party Providers

We use selected third-party providers for individual technical functions:

  • Google Firebase: authentication, database, sync, and technical services
  • Apple HealthKit: access to health and fitness data only with your consent
  • AI services: processing AI chat messages to generate responses

6. Transfers to Third Countries

Personal data may be transferred to third countries, such as the United States, as part of the services we use. Where required, such transfers are protected by appropriate safeguards such as Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs).

7. Data Security

We protect data through TLS transmission, encryption, and technical access restrictions. Access is limited to authorized and authenticated requests.

8. Retention Period

Data is stored as long as your account is active. After account deletion, related data is deleted within 30 days unless statutory retention obligations apply.

9. Your Rights

Under the GDPR, you have the following rights:

  • access to stored personal data
  • correction of inaccurate data
  • deletion of personal data
  • restriction of processing
  • data portability
  • objection to certain processing activities

To exercise your rights, contact us at fit.data1@gmail.com.

10. Automated Decision-Making

We do not use automated decision-making, including profiling, that produces legal effects concerning you or similarly significantly affects you.

11. Children

The app is not intended for children under 13, therefore no personal data from children under 13 is collected.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy if legal, technical, or functional requirements change. Significant changes will be communicated in an appropriate manner.
